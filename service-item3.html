WEB DESIGN:

What Makes a Good Website ?

When it comes to website design, studies have shown that users quickly judge your business based on visuals alone, and will often stop using your site if it’s poorly designed.

Purpose

Great design starts with a purpose in mind.
Ask yourself, “What do I want to accomplish with this page?”

Aesthetically pleasing.

A visually appealing website is easy to digest, more functional, enjoyable, and inviting.

Simplicity

Simplicity is the best way to go when considering the user experience and the usability of your website.
Putting too many elements on the page may lead to distracting visitors from the main purpose of your website.
