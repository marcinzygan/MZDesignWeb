POSTER DESIGN:

What Makes a Good Poster ?

A poster is supposed to catch the attention, inform, convince and provoke.
If the viewer feels addressed, a poster can influence his or her decisions through text and images.

Is it Effective ?

To be effective, a poster should :

Attract attention.

Be memorable.

Have a clearly structured message or statement.

MY DESIGN PROCESS:


6 Steps of Poster Design
Design Brief

01

I will need some information about the project in order to come up with the perfect poster for you.

To do this, I will send you a research questionnaire, which has been designed to gain a good understanding of you, your business, competition and target audience.


Reserch

02

I will make my own reserch to find out more about your buisness and industry .


Design Concepts

03

I will spend some time on brainstorming and idea-generation.

I will get my initial ideas down on paper and sketch out rough concepts.

Once ideas have been explored I will begin to work on the computer design.


Review and Refine

04

I will take a step back from work , and look at it later.

This will allow me to get fresh perspective and I can refine and perfect the designs where needed.


Presentation

05

Once design is ready to present, I will create a PDF document which will display the poster design.

This will include a poster with few color variations , and also visualisation in real life use , like bus stops , banners , billboards , etc .

After presentation we will discuss any changes to the design .


Final Delivery

06

Once a poster is complete and approved , I wll create the final design files.
This will include :
Print Files
Web Files
JPEG files
I will also transfer full copyright of design to you.
