LOGO DESIGN:

What Makes a Good Logo ?

A great logo is appropriate, memorable and simple in form.

Is it Appropriate?

Appropriate means that the logo is relevant in form and concept to the client and its industry.

Is it Simple?

Simple means that a logo has to be focused on a single story and in most cases it must be uncomplicated in form.

Is it Memorable?

Memorable means that while the form must be simple, it must also be distinctive enough to be easily remembered.

MY DESIGN PROCESS:


My Designing Process
Logo Design Brief

01

I will need some information about the project in order to come up with the perfect logo for you.

To do this, I will send you a research questionnaire, which has been designed to gain a good understanding of you, your business, competition and target audience.


Reserch

02

I will make my own reserch to find out more about your buisness and industry .


Design Concepts

03

I will spend some time on brainstorming and idea-generation.

I will get my initial ideas down on paper and sketch out rough concepts.

Once ideas have been explored I will begin to work on the computer design.


Review and Refine

04

I will take a step back from work , and look at it later.

This will allow me to get fresh perspective and I can refine and perfect the designs where needed.


Presentation

05

Once design is ready to present, I will create a PDF document which will display the logo design.

This will include a logo with few color variations , and also visualisation of the logo in real life use , like buisness cards , store banners etc .

After presentation we will discuss any changes to the design .


Final Delivery

06

Once a logo is complete and approved , I wll create the final design files.
This will include :
Print Files
Web Files
JPEG files
PNG images
I will also transfer full copyright of design to you.
